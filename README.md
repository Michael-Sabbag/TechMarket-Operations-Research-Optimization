# TechMarket Ltd - Operations Research Optimization Project

## Overview

This project presents an Operations Research optimization study developed for **TechMarket Ltd**. The objective is to analyze the company's production and business processes using mathematical modeling and optimization techniques in order to maximize profitability while respecting operational constraints.

The project demonstrates how Operations Research can be applied to real-world business problems by combining mathematical functions, data analysis, and Microsoft Excel.

---

## Project Objectives

The main objectives of this project are to:

- Analyze the company's monthly revenue performance.
- Build mathematical revenue and cost models.
- Calculate and maximize company profit.
- Study production efficiency and operational costs.
- Apply partial derivatives to identify optimal production strategies.
- Demonstrate optimization techniques commonly used in Operations Research.

---

## Mathematical Models

The project includes several mathematical concepts:

### Revenue Function

The revenue generated by the company is modeled as a mathematical function depending on production variables.

### Cost Function

Production costs are represented using linear and quadratic functions to simulate realistic manufacturing expenses.

### Profit Function

Profit is calculated as:

\[
Profit = Revenue - Cost
\]

The objective is to maximize this function while satisfying operational constraints.

### Partial Derivatives

Partial derivatives are used to determine the optimal production levels by locating the critical points of the profit function.

### Constraint Optimization

Business constraints such as production capacity, available resources, and operational limits are considered during the optimization process.
